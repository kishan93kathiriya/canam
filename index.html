Our Mission

At Can-Am Physical Therapy & Rehab our mission is to provide individualized care to everyone regardless of age and ability. We empower you to become independent and successful with your functional goals using science-backed therapeutic techniques.

Our Vision

Movement Without Pain, Dependence, or Limitations. Everyone deserves the ability to move freely and confidently. True health is the restoration of function, strength, and control that allows you to participate fully in daily life.

Ankit Patel

Physiotherapist Clinic Owner

Ankit has worked in the fitness and rehabilitation industry for many years. He brings a wealth of diverse experience, having worked in gym settings, with young athletes, the Olympic speed skaters' team, and ballet dancers. His career spans various environments including nursing homes, home care, prisons, and specialized clinics.

He established his own clinic to prioritize exceptional manual therapy and provide a dedicated one-on-one, hands-on experience for every client. His primary goal is to help patients gain functional independence and significantly improve their quality of life.
