Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

At Bones and Balance Physical Therapy & Rehab our mission is to provide individualized care to everyone regardless of age, and abilities to become independent and successful with their functional goals using various therapeutic techniques, backed by sciences and researches.

Our Vision

Movement Without Pain, Dependence, or Limitations.

Everyone deserves the ability to move freely, confidently, and within their own physical capabilities—without relying on supportive or pain-masking medications. True health is not simply the absence of pain, but the restoration of function, strength, balance, and control that allows individuals to participate fully in their daily lives.

Comprehensive Care

Specialized Rehabilitation Services

We offer a wide range of evidence-based treatments tailored to your specific needs, from neurological conditions to sports injuries.

Neuro Rehabilitation

Specialized care for Stroke, Multiple Sclerosis (MS), ALS, and Parkinson's disease. We focus on neuroplasticity and functional independence.

  • Stroke Recovery
  • Parkinson's Management
  • MS & ALS Support

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Safe, monitored exercise programs to improve heart health and endurance following cardiac events or for prevention.

  • Post-MI Recovery
  • Heart Failure Management
  • Prevention Programs

Home Care Services

We bring the clinic to you. Professional rehabilitation services provided in the comfort of your own home.

  • Post-Surgical Home Visits
  • Geriatric Mobility
  • Convenient & Personalized

Workplace Analysis

Professional ergonomic assessments and injury prevention strategies tailored for your workplace environment.

  • Desk Setup Optimization
  • Injury Prevention Training
  • Return-to-Work Programs

Advanced Therapy

Specialized hands-on techniques including IASTM, Functional Dry Needling, and Manual Therapy for pain relief and mobility.

  • Joint Mobilization
  • Soft Tissue Release
  • Dry Needling

Vestibular Rehab

Treatment for dizziness, vertigo, and balance disorders to restore stability and confidence in movement.

  • BPPV Treatment
  • Gait Training
  • Fall Prevention
  • Concussion Treatments
